Saravana V

Saravana is a 2006 Indian Tamil-language action romance film directed by K. S. Ravikumar.[1] It stars Silambarasan, Jyothika, Five Star Krishna, Vivek, and Prakash Raj. The film was a high-budget production under Deivanai Movies and the soundtrack was composed by Srikanth Deva. It is the Tamil remake of 2005 Telugu film, Bhadra.[2] Saravana was released on 14 January 2006, coinciding with Pongal and became a commercial success at the box office.
